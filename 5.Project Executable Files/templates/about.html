RAILWAY SENTRY: Detecting Workers On Railway Track

Our Story

Railway Sentry is an advanced safety system designed to detect and monitor workers on railway tracks using the state-of-the-art YOLO V9 object detection algorithm.

  • Worker Safety Monitoring
  • Trespasser Detection
  • Wildlife Detection

The primary objective of Railway Sentry is to enhance safety measures and prevent accidents by swiftly identifying the presence of workers or unauthorized individuals in railway track areas.

Railway Sentry: Detecting Workers on Railway Tracks using YOLO V9

The primary objective of Railway Sentry is to enhance safety measures and prevent accidents by swiftly identifying the presence of workers or unauthorized individuals in railway track areas.

Worker Safety Monitoring

Railway workers are frequently required to perform maintenance, repairs, or inspections along railway tracks, putting them at risk of accidents from oncoming trains

Trespasser Detection

Railway Sentry is equipped with YOLO V9 to detect unauthorized individuals or trespassers entering railway track areas.

Wildlife Detection

Railway Sentry employs YOLO V9 to detect the presence of wildlife such as deer, bears, or birds on railway tracks

Surveillance and Monitoring

Remote Track Surveillance: Uses cameras and sensors along the tracks to monitor remote or unmanned areas for any activity.
